BODC Trading & Investment Company Limited is a proudly Nigerian organization incorporated in February 2010 as a private limited liability company with the mandate to operate as a trading and investment company.

Our Corporate Objectives

We are dedicated to the quest of improving the lives of Nigerians by facilitating their access to the everyday essentials of life, through a combination of human and material resources deployed strategically across various vital industrial sectors.

Our Registered Activities

BODC is registered to perform the following activities, among others:

  • To carry on business as a general commercial company;
  • To carry on the business of a trading and investment company;
  • To establish factories, industries, and farms, for the production of raw materials, articles, and general goods;
  • To establish warehouse, offices, stores, supermarkets and other premises in Nigeria and elsewhere for the display, promotion and distribution of all or any of the foregoing purposes.

Our Shareholding STatus

Our initial authorized share capital is Ten Million Naira (N10,000,000.00).

This authorized share capital has been issued and allotted with only N4 million called and paid up as at April 30, 2010.

Corporate Values

As an organization, we believe the following principles, and the values they represent guide our existence and conduct every day, in every way:

Safety...

We strongly value the human life and the sanctity safety of the environment in which he lives and thrives. Thus, we are committed to the utmost safety and security of man and society.

Integrity...

We believe in honesty being the best policy. Hence, we are open, sincere and even-handed in our dealings with all stakeholders.

Teamwork...

We trust in, and rely on the time-honored and unfailing tenet of teamwork. Therefore, we are dedicated to making the most of man, materials and technology to achieve the best for the people and society.

Diligence...

We believe in service. Our enormous dedicated approach to work, our responsibilities, our customers, our staff, our partners, and the society we serve is deeply rooted in our resolute sense of duty.

Excellence...

We believe in distinction. Thus we always go the extra mile, and work a little harder to achieve goals bigger than the ordinary; heights higher than the average; and deliver superior values that meet and exceed expectations at all times.
