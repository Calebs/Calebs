Changing Face of Lagos

Changing Face of Lagos, is an initiative of Ibile Foundation, Lagos, (The Foundation for FutureLagos), that seeks to capture and showcase the completed and ongoing projects of the Akinwunmi Ambode administration since taking on the mantle of leadership on May 29, 2015, as well as create a citizens engagement platform, online, mobile and in app to facilitate communication between Lagosians and the government, on various topics and matters that matter to all Lagosians.
