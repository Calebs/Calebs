A vintage scarf inspired the geometric pattern on our flared Textured Burlap Skirt. This quintessential day-to-evening style is softly tailored in a tweed-like cotton blend that’s lightweight, with an especially elegant drape. It’s fully lined for a smooth, comfortable fit and features yoke seams — a stitching detail that adds flattering volume and movement to the silhouette.

  • A-line
  • Invisible zipper with hook and eye closure at center back.
  • 18" (45.2 cm) in length; hits above knee.
  • Textured burlap (78% cotton, 12% viscose, 10% polyester).
  • Dry clean.
