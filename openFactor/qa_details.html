Software Testing & Quality Assurance Service

OpenFactor offers full-cycle testing and quality assurance services for enterprise applications that includes core banking, CRM, ERP and Bespoke applications.

We have team of experts with domain experience in banking, insurance, payment industry. We take the QA nuance out of every IT project to ensure the deliverables meet the most stringent quality standards.

Why OpenFactor?

Quality Assurance Experts

OpenFactor’s dedicated QA unit employs highly experienced quality assurance professionals, including ISTQB®-certified engineers. We are highly motivated, results-oriented, and have a passion for constantly improving our processes and workflows.

Comprehensive QA Knowledge

Our engineers are well-versed in all the standard test types and beyond. Whether testing functionality, performance, UI, or localization, conducting security audits, or implementing complex test automation.

100% Transparency and Control

Our approach relies on trust and 100% results visibility. You are always in control, with full access to comprehensive QA reporting, which includes test results, test coverage, quality level and status reports, quality trends, sign-off reports, and more.

Business Domain Expertise

Our QA team has accumulated a wealth of business domain experience. Our particular forte is Banking, Insurance, Retail, Energy, Health etc. With proven record of success in delivery of cutting edge and transformational technology solutions and services across the industry

Our Approach

OpenFactor’s client engagement is driven by our tested and proven delivery model Assess, Design, Execute and Monitor - ADEM model. This delivery model is the backbone of our successful project delivery.

We utilize contemporary tools like Selenium, LoadRunner, Ranorex, Cucumber and DevOps methodology that guarantees continuous delivery high quality software application that delivers business value.
