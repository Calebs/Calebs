Is this a Learning Management System or an Interactive Content Platform?

Gradely combines the best of both worlds. We are also the only LMS provider that offer curriculum-aligned interactive content for schools in Nigeria.

What subjects, classes and curriculum does your LMS cover?

We currently have questions for Mathematics, English Language and Biology for all classes and the Nigerian and British curriculums. All other subjects will be available as time goes on.

We have video lessons for Mathematics, English Language, Agricultural Science, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Further Mathematics, Accounting, Commerce, Civic education, CRK, Economics, French, Literature-in-English and Yoruba/Hausa/Igbo for Senior Secondary classes. Videos for other classes are in production.

Does your platform have an administrative management system?

No, we don’t have an administrative management system. We only have academic support for now but there are free and low-cost administrative system out there we can recommend to you.

What are the pricing packages for Gradely for Schools?

Gradely for Schools LMS cost NGN 6000 per student per year of LMS subscription: this covers premium access to all features of the LMS including Interactive Class Feed, Live Video Class, Assessment tool with built-in question pool, Proctored Examination and Reporting.

Gradely CatchUp! is our personalised content bank of video lessons and practice cost N 12,000 per student per year.

How do you protect school data on Gradely?

We adhere to GDPR standards of data privacy and individual data protection. Also, we ensure there is server-side transmission encryption. All uploaded files are stored on file storage platforms for performance and security. All files have unique token assign to them at creation for access, therefore cannot be guessed or read by humans.

Every single request on our platform requires authentication, meaning we’ll have to know who you are before we determine what you get.
