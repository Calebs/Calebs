Conversations at
the Gradely Launch

Edtech Leaders

Boye Oshinaga, Gradely’s CEO hosts a conversation with the leaders of other top edtech companies Titilayo (CEO, 9ijakids), Henry (CEO, Schoolable) and CEO of Nurture House, Ayopeju Njideaka . This wide-ranging discussion touches on innovation for education, how technology can truly power the classroom for the better, and ways to overcome its limitations.

Parent community

Seyi Adelaju, Gradely’s Head of Growth hosts a conversation with parenting thought leaders Foluso Gbadamosi, Kola Aina and Morayo Osiyemi on the new role technology has created for parents in the classroom, parents’ expectations from school and the opportunity for home schooling.
