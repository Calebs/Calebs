Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you offer?

We accept all major credit cards and direct debit. We also support transactions in a range of currencies.

How do I switch between plans?

You can switch between the plans at any time by going to the subscription page in your account and changing your plan. Make a selection and we’ll tell you the price based on the number of children you have.

Is the free plan free forever?

Yes, it is. As long as you have 2 or fewer children. You can use all the features in the free plan without paying us a dime. We won’t even ask for a credit card.

How many children can I add if I use the Basic plan?

The Basic plan is limited to three children. If you need to add more children, you need to purchase the premium plan. The basic plan was built for regular families with an average of 2 childeen. The premium plan offers a more robust feature set including expanded curriculum and advance analytics.

Can I cancel a plan after payment?

You will be notified 30 days before your annual plan is set to renew. At this time, you can change the number of seats on your plan; otherwise, it will renew for the same number of seats again. You could also choose not to renew your plan.
