What are the technical requirements for Gradely?

All you need to get started is a computer, tablet, or phone with a connection to the Internet.

Is Gradely curriculum compliant?

Yes. Gradely is built by learning specialists guided by the WAEC / NECO as well as the British IGCSE curriculum.

How much does Gradely CatchUp! cost?

Registration is free. You can create one free diagnostic test for your child. Our basic subscription plan of #1,500 gives your child access to Gradely gamified practice for one month. You'll also get notifications on your child's learning progress every time.

How will my child use Gradely?

After you signup, you'll be required to connect with your child on Gradely. You can do this by creating a profile for your child from your Gradely dashboard. As soon as this is done, you will receive the login details for your child. Your child can login via https://gradely.ng/login using the student code and password that you've generated.

How does live tutoring work on Gradely?

A learning specialist will be assigned to your child based on the report of the diagnostic test. A link to connect to the tutor via video chat will be sent to you via email as well as the other information you'll need after booking a session.

How much does a live tutoring session cost?

#5,000. We're currently piloting this service and it is free for now. Take this offer today.

Do I need multiple parents account if I have more than one child?

Not at all. You only need a single parent account to use Gradely. You can add as many students as you want from your dashboard on Gradely.
