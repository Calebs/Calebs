Agribusiness Capacity Building Sessions

Get support to identify, develop and launch market driven, investor ready Agricultural Technology Solutions.

Apply Now

Existing and Intending Agtech entrepreneurs, like many technology entrepreneurs in Nigeria, face numerous challenges such as insufficient knowledge and skills, inadequate investment and financing, and limited services offered by technology hubs and entrepreneurship ecosystem institutions. There appears to be little on-going support for entrepreneurs using technology to solve challenges in the agriculture sector.

The Agtech Incubation program is designed to help interested tech entrepreneurs evolve into focused Agtech entrepreneurs by supporting them with tailored incubation services that will enable them develop solutions, market offerings and business models that address real constraints identified by farmers & actual agribusinesses in the frontlines and increase the likelihood of accessing finance or attracting investment, prior to launching their start-ups. The goal is to ensure that your Agtech solutions are relevant, and emerge onto the market under a monetisable business model that can be taken to scale and see strong market demand from the start. The Agtech incubation program promise:

The Agtech Incubation Program is packed full with benefits

Discover real opportunities identified by farmers & actual agribusinesses in the frontlines.

Evolve relevant, profitable and scalable AgTech solutions that addresses specific constraints

Benefit from robust and iterative stakeholder consultation and feedback mechanisms when developing and testing your agtech solutions

Craft valuable offerings at appropriate sophistication levels and at the right cost to the farmer or agribusiness

Build monetizable business models, realistic for adoption, attractive to, and understood by investors.

Get increased access to finance and learn best practices
