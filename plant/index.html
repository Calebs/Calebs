Transforming Agriculture through Technology

The PLANT program exists to to foster the identification, development and promotion of technology innovations that address real agriculture / agribusines constraints at the right cost and sophistication level and tackle the challenges that prohibit agribusinesses, farmers and other agri-value chain businesses from adopting these solutions at scale

Proudly Supported / Implemented by

co-creating and scaling ag-tech innovation

The AgTech Interface
Network

The Agtech Interface Network connects agribusinesses, tech entrepreneurs, investors, business schools, business advisors and other relevant stakeholders to foster an ecosystem that supports the generation and adoption of technological innovation in agriculture.

Agtech Entrepreneurs

Discover real opportunities for tech-innovation as identified by practicing agribusinesses, launch innovative start-ups, get access to finance and learn industry best practices.

Agri Business Owners

Get more from your efforts, by learning & collaborating with experts, customers, enablers and other stakeholders.

Agri-Investors

Gain contextual awareness of the agribusiness sector to enable you recognize and understand real Agtech investment opportunities.

Incubation Hubs

Receive advisory services to improve your offerings to Agtech entrepreneurs.

Education & Research Institutions

Provide business planning and financial modelling services to AgTech startups and evolving agribusinesses.

Agribusiness Stakeholders

A capacity building program designed to provide training and advisory services to agribusinesses in the south of Nigeria, to improve their business operations while simultaneously building.

Building the AgTech Ecosystem

PLANT is Supporting different players across the agtech ecosystem through various tailored interventions

Incubator/Accelerator Support

Providing advisory services to help incubators and accelerators improve their offerings to tech entrepreneurs interested in the agriculture sector.

Learn More

AgTech Entrepreneurs Incubation

Providing business incubation support to intending and existing agtech entrepreneurs to enable them identify, develop and launch market driven, investor ready Agricultural Technology Solutions.

Learn More

Agribusiness Capacity Building Sessions

Supporting agri business platers to develop deeper understanding of the agricultural value chains, and get connected to innovative technologies that can increase their reach, profitability & sustainability
