The Agtech Interface Network

Interact, share knowledge, and form partnerships to build and scale innovative, sustainable tech driven solutions to priority challenges in the agricultural sector.

The AgTech Interface Network is our ecosystem that supports the identification, development and widespread adoption of innovative agricultural technologies that address real problems and opportunities identified by practicing agribusinesses and farmers.

Agtech Entrepreneurs

Discover real opportunities for tech-innovation as identified by practicing agribusinesses, launch innovative start-ups, get access to finance and learn industry best practices.

Agri Business Owners

Get more from your efforts, by learning & collaborating with experts, customers, enablers and other stakeholders.

Agri-Investors

Gain contextual awareness of the agribusiness sector to enable you recognize and understand real Agtech investment opportunities.

Incubation Hubs

Receive advisory services to improve your offerings to Agtech entrepreneurs.

Education & Research Institutions

Provide business planning and financial modelling services to AgTech startups and evolving agribusinesses.

Agribusiness Stakeholders

A capacity building program designed to provide training and advisory services to agribusinesses in the south of Nigeria, to improve their business operations while simultaneously building.

How does the AIN operate?

The AIN operates by connecting agribusinesses, technology innovators, investors, and other stakeholders to generate and scale new technological innovations in agriculture.

Through a series of events and activities, the AIN will provide sound and practical understanding of the realities of agricultural value chains, and a basic understanding of the relevant innovative technologies, so that they can recognise opportunities, understand the context, and become excited about agriculture and Agtech.

This should ensure that technologies coming to the market see strong market demand from the start and emerge onto the market under a monetisable business model that can be taken to scale.
