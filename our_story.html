Our Story

We’ve been “Changing the Face of Lagos” since May 29, 2015. We’re passionate about Lagos, its 5 divisions and about the potential that lies in our great state. We believe that the future of Lagos, and indeed Nigeria, is filled with opportunity. The Akinwunmi Ambode Administration is striving to create this growth in partnership with all Lagosians

Our Spirit Statement

Our spirit statement (vision) is to become the benchmark of socio-economic development in Nigeria. We’re in the business of building FutureLagos, where every citizen, every resident, and every visitor matters.

We believe that – in addition to our ongoing projects and comments from citizens – the proof of our performance impact lies in our ability to fulfill our dreams for FutureLagos.

Our Soul Statement

Our soul statement (mission) is to positively shape the future of Lagos . We have a purpose that is greater than us and we hope to contribute to it in every way we can. We want to uplift the lives of people, and be the catalyst for success in every area of our state. We believe that this begins with enabling healthy individuals, who build healthy communities, healthy businesses and a healthy state .

Our Values

Shape the culture and behaviour of Lagosians. The vital values that, that drive the work of Changing Face of Lagos are:

  • Authenticity
  • Fulfilling campaign promises.
  • Growth & Development mindset.
  • Listening to the people /Citizens engagement.
  • Loyalty.

Our Pledge

I pledge to Lagos my State to be forthright, dedicated and honest, to serve the State with all my passion, to promote the welfare of its people regardless of age, gender, tribe or religion, and to enhance its status, through good governance, selflessness and accountability. So help me God!

I Akinwunmi Ambode invite you all, Lagosians and non-Lagosians alike, boys and girls, young and old, men and women, those physically able and those with challenges; I invite you all regardless of your tribe, cultural background and religious persuasion; I invite you to help me and the government I hope to establish, with your support, and the help of God, to build the Lagos of our dreams.

Forward Ever!!!

Changing Face of Lagos

Changing Face of Lagos is an initiative by the Office of Governor of Lagos and Ibile Foundation (The Foundation for FutureLagos).

Driven by the motto; “Preserve the best of the past, Strive for quality today and Plan properly for the future” our mission is to showcase the achievements of the Akinwunmi Ambode Administration since 2015 and promote planned initiatives as well as new initiatives to be implemented over the next five years the for the FutureLagos that is emerging.

Working in partnership with the Office of the Governor, the Ibile Foundation team of Consultants, Futurists, Designers (web/graphics), Content Creators, Project Managers work assiduously using multi-platforms

Changing Face of Lagos propagates Lagos State’s economic and investment opportunities, tourism potentials, entertainment and sports, immense arts and cultural heritage, and her wide range of hospitality, culinary and leisure experiences.

We also arrange events and visits to a variety of places, buildings and institutions for potential investors and visitors to Lagos.

T.H.E.S.E
Tourism, Hospitality, Entertainment & Sporting Excellence.

In contrast to Napoleon Bonaparte, who once said that “the best way to keep one’s word is not to give it”, an uncompromising penchant for keeping his word has become the trade mark of Lagos State Governor, Mr. Akinwunmi Ambode.

One of his campaign promises was to ensure that Lagos becomes the tourism and entertainment hub of Africa. This he hopes to achieve through T.H.E.S.E. which is an acronym for Tourism, Hospitality, Entertainment and Sporting Excellence. With the current economic downturns in the country, the pursuit of T.H.E.S.E. has become an urgent necessity. Hence, Governor Ambode has left no one in doubt about his resolve to grow the Lagos economy and empower the youth through several alternative means which include the tactical promotion of tourism, entertainment and sports.

T.H.E.S.E 2017, no doubt, would further provide a huge boost for the promotion of Ambode administration’s T.H.E.S.E. policy thrust. With the impending Golden Jubilee anniversary of the state, later this year, the art, entertainment, tourism and other such related sectors are, once again, certainly in for a great deal. This, indeed, is the whole essence of T.H.E.S.E

Fola Adeyemi,Permanent Secretary, Lagos State Ministry of Information & Strategy

Ibile Foundation

Ibile Foundation Ltd/GTE is a non-government organisation committed to profering solutions to complex challenges affecting Lagos and supporting the initiatives of the State government and stakeholders. Since its inception in 2007 Ibile Foundation has been involved in the deployment of numerous privately funded initiatives to promote Lagos, which has earned it the title “The Foundation for Lagos”.

Our goal is to support HE, Governor Akinwunmi Ambode administration in the quest to transform Lagos into a 21st century cosmopolitan city in the heart of Africa and Africa’s most powerful city brand.

Lagos and Partners

Lagos and Partners is the PR and empowerment marketing division of Ibile Foundation. Core practice areas include Leadership and Governance, Consulting, Citizens Engagement Solutions, Perception and Image Management, Campaign and Digital Media Solutions.

We believe in strategic partnerships and work with the best companies and professionals in country and across the globe to deliver world class place branding and destination marketing services for Lagos.

Lagos and Partners is registered in the United Kingdom to manage international marketing for Lagos in the UK and beyond. Our mission is to promote Lagos to global audiences.
