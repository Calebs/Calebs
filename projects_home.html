iLoveLagos

iLoveLagos is about the city of Lagos, Nigeria. Africa’s own big apple. A vibrant mega-city and confluence of people always on the go with a can-do way of life. Lagos is the very place to be in Africa, and it is the intention of the Lagos State government to make Lagos one of the top five tourist destinations in Africa. iLoveLagos opens up the sights, sounds and tastes of Lagos to visitors from around the world.

Secure Lagos

Secure Lagos is targeted at identifying and overcoming physical and intelligence security vulnerabilities within Lagos, and preservation of social infrastructure through civic engagement. The portal allows Lagos citizens to report infrastructural breakdown and security threats for prompt intervention by the relevant ministries and agencies.

LagosGo

LagosGO is a dedicated tourism portal that shows tourists where to stay, things to do, what to eat, and where to go in Lagos. LagosGO also provides visitors with special offers and deals on new and trending attractions, events and accommodation, giving tourists an explorer’s unforgettable Lagos experience.
