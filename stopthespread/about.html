About #StoptheSpread

The Agtech Incubation program is designed to help interested tech entrepreneurs evolve into focused Agtech entrepreneurs by supporting them with tailored incubation services that will enable them develop solutions, market offerings and business models that address real constraints identified by farmers & actual agribusinesses in the frontlines and increase the likelihood of accessing finance or attracting investment, prior to launching their start-ups
